# Librarian Dashboard

## Purpose

This dashboard helps me use the Performance Brain Librarian correctly.

The Librarian helps me turn raw information into structured Obsidian notes, links, tags, metadata, and decision rules.

---

## Current Librarian Workflow

1. Capture raw information
2. Send it to the Librarian
3. Librarian classifies the information
4. Librarian drafts the note or update
5. I review it
6. I paste it into Obsidian
7. I connect it to related notes
8. I update workflows if needed

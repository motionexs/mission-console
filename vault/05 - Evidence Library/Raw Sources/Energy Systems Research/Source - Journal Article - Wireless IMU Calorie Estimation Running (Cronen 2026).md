# Source - Journal Article - Wireless IMU Calorie Estimation Running (Cronen 2026)

## Source Type
Journal Article

## Full Citation
Cronen A, Moriarty M, Lee-Confer J, Renner K. Validation of a Wireless Device-Driven Method of Estimating Caloric Expenditure during Running. Applied Physiology, Nutrition, and Metabolism. 2026 Jun 22. doi:10.1139/apnm-2025-0395. PMID: 42330544.

## Direct Link
- DOI: https://doi.org/10.1139/apnm-2025-0395
- PubMed: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42330544

## Main Topic
Estimation of caloric expenditure (energy cost) during running using wearable IMU sensors.

## Key Ideas
- Two wireless IMUs (8.5 g each, $250 total) can estimate energy expenditure compared to breath-by-breath respirometry.
- Average RMSE of 2.16 +/- 1.50 W/kg during maximal exercise protocol.
- Demonstrates feasibility of affordable, wireless, field-based metabolic monitoring.

## Practical Coaching Meaning
- Provides a low-cost, field-friendly tool for estimating aerobic energy cost during running sessions.
- Can be used to monitor training intensity and progression without lab equipment.
